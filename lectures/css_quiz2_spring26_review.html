Review Questions

Question 1: (1 pt) Which of the following is the best description of zero shot prompting?

    1. Asking the LLM a question with no system prompt
    1. Sending a prompt that contains zero words
    1. Asking the LLM to perform a task without providing any examples of the desired output

    1. The bot may make up an answer about delivery rather than saying it doesn’t know
    1. The bot will crash because it can’t answer geography questions
    1. The context window will overflow

    1. Zero shot prompting

    1. The LLM itself, based on the conversation

Question 7: (2 pts) You are building a few shot prompt to rewrite formal sentences into casual ones. Which example set is best?

    1. “The weather is nice today. / I like weather.”

Question 9: (2 pts) An LLM is asked: “A train leaves Chicago at 9am traveling at 60mph. Another leaves New York at 10am traveling at 80mph toward Chicago. The distance between them is 790 miles. When do they meet? Work through each step.” What technique is being used?

Question 12: (2 pts) A prompt chain has three steps. Step 1 produces a list of keywords. Step 2 writes an article using those keywords. Step 3 checks the article for grammar. There is a major error in Step 1’s keyword list. What happens?

    1. Step 2 and Step 3 will automatically detect and fix the error
    1. The error only affects Step 1; the other steps are independent
